A Few Words About Me

I’m currently working as a Software Engineer at Newgen Software Technologies  where I am writing Java and Angular to build workflow management products used in organisations.

Before starting to work full time, I interned with Newgen Software Technologies as a Software Engineering Intern and graduated in the Summer of 2018 from Shiv Nadar University, Dadri, India.

Out of interest and curiosity, I have (little) experience with Designing Products and Interfaces through my pursuit of a Minor Degree in Design during my undergrad at Shiv Nadar University. I have studied a few MOOCs on User Interface and User Experience Design. Also, I earned a User Interface Design Specialization through MOOCs offered by University of Minnesota listed on Coursera.

I live and work in Delhi-NCR, India. I get adrenaline rush when coding games as my side projects. I like to sketch and illustrate digital art works. I am a long distance running junkie.
